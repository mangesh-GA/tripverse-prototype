📜 Refund Policy

🚌 Bus Tickets & Passes

• Cancelled 24h+ before travel: 100% refund

• Cancelled 4–24h before: 50% refund

• Cancelled less than 4h / No-show: No refund

🚆 Train Tickets

• More than 48h before departure: Full refund – ₹50 charge

• 12–48h before: 50% refund

• Less than 4h / after departure: No refund

✈️ Flight Tickets

• More than 7 days before: Full refund – ₹500 fee

• 24h–7 days before: 75% refund

• Less than 24h: Non-refundable

💡 Refunds are credited to the original payment method within 5–7 business days. For UPI refunds, credit is typically within 24 hours.
