The Story

From Frustrated Commuter
to Tech Founder

Mangesh Gaikwad grew up in Pune — a city that thrives on movement. Every morning, millions of students, professionals, and tourists navigate a sprawling web of buses, trains, and flights. But for years, this navigation required three separate apps, physical passes, paper tickets, and countless hours of confusion.

During his engineering days at Pune University, Mangesh experienced first-hand the frustration of missing a bus because he hadn't bought the right pass, losing money on non-refundable tickets, and having no reliable way to know if his train was delayed. He saw the same problem echoed in thousands of his fellow commuters.

"Why do I need five apps to travel from my college to the airport? There has to be a better way."

That question became the seed of TripVerse. In 2024, after two years of building and iterating, Mangesh launched the first version of TripVerse — a unified platform where students could get subsidized digital bus passes, professionals could manage corporate travel, and tourists could hop between modes of transport with a single account.

The reception was beyond expectations. Within six months, TripVerse had processed over 200,000 bookings across Maharashtra. Word spread through college campuses and corporate offices alike. The platform's real-time crowd prediction feature — Mangesh's personal favourite — became one of the most talked-about innovations in Indian transit technology.

Today, TripVerse covers 580+ cities, serves 2.4 million monthly travelers, and is on a mission to become the operating system of Indian travel. Mangesh leads a team of 40 engineers, designers, and operations specialists with one shared belief: smart travel is a fundamental right, not a luxury.

📅 The TripVerse Journey

2022
The Idea is Born
Mangesh identifies the fragmented state of urban transport apps in India and begins research and user interviews across 5 cities.
2023
First Prototype Built
A team of 3 engineers builds the first working prototype covering bus passes and train tickets. 500 beta users onboarded at Fergusson College, Pune.
Early 2024
Seed Round Raised
TripVerse raises ₹2.5 Cr seed funding from angel investors to expand to 10 cities and build the flight booking module.
Mid 2024
Public Launch & 200K Bookings
Official launch in Mumbai and Pune. Platform processes 200,000 bookings in first 6 months. Live status and crowd prediction features debut.
2025
Series A & National Expansion
TripVerse closes Series A round and expands to 200+ cities. Corporate pass program launches for enterprise clients. Team grows to 40.
2026
2.4M Monthly Travelers & Growing
TripVerse now covers 580+ cities, 12,000+ routes, and is India's fastest-growing unified travel platform. International expansion underway.
"India has the world's largest railway network, one of the fastest-growing aviation markets, and hundreds of millions of daily bus commuters. Yet we've never had a single platform that unifies all three. TripVerse exists to change that — to give every Indian traveler the intelligence, convenience, and dignity they deserve, whether they're a student taking the 7 AM bus or a CEO catching a midnight flight."
— Mangesh Gaikwad, Founder & CEO, TripVerse
