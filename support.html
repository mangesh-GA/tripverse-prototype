❓ Frequently Asked Questions

How do I get my digital QR bus pass? ▼

After completing registration and payment, your QR pass is emailed instantly. You can also view it in your Dashboard under QR Pass. Tap it at the bus reader to board — no paper needed.

How long does a refund take? ▼

Refunds are processed within 5–7 business days for cards/net banking. UPI refunds usually credit within 24 hours. Track every step on the Refunds page.

Can I change my travel route after buying a bus pass? ▼

One route change is allowed per month for Student and Professional passes. Submit a route change request via your Dashboard and it will be applied within 24 hours.

My bus pass QR doesn't scan — what do I do? ▼

Show the driver your booking confirmation SMS or email. Then contact our support chat immediately — we'll troubleshoot and issue a replacement QR within 1 hour, guaranteed.

Can I book tickets for someone else? ▼

Yes! Enter the passenger's details during booking. The QR pass will carry their name and can be shared directly to their phone via WhatsApp or email.

Is my payment information secure? ▼

Absolutely. TripVerse uses 256-bit SSL encryption and is PCI-DSS Level 1 compliant. We never store card details on our servers. All payments go through certified payment gateways.

How does the crowd prediction feature work? ▼

Our AI model analyzes historical boarding data, time of day, day of week, and local events to predict crowd levels with ~87% accuracy. Plan your trip during off-peak hours for a comfortable seat.

I haven't received my booking confirmation email. ▼

First, check your spam/junk folder. If it's not there, log in to your Dashboard — all bookings appear there. You can also resend the confirmation from the trip details screen or contact support.
